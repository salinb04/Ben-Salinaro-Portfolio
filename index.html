About Me

My name is Ben Salinaro, and I am an upbeat, solutions-savvy Full Stack Web Developer. After graduating from the University of Texas, I worked at Imaginuity, a digital marketing agency, for 2.5 years as a UX Designer and created many polished, fully functional, interactive prototypes of websites for clients. I recently completed the SMU full-stack Web Dev bootcamp with a focus on the MERN stack.
